Katy & Grant's Sawyer, Michigan Engagement

This past February Katy and Grant Sawyer got engaged and wanted their engagement photographs to capture their small town roots.

Although they both live in Chicago, we decided to utilize a weekend trip to take their photographs in Ann Harbor, Michigan. The setting allowed for some fantastic results with images that capture a more rural and intimate setting.

Location weddings and engagements are always an exciting opportunity for me to capture environments and areas that you simply cannot find in the areas surrounding Chicago.
