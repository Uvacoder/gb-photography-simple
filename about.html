Hi, I'm Graham Buss.
I'm a photographer currently living in Chicago.

By this point, you know I'm a wedding photographer, but that's just part of who will be taking your pictures. I live with my beautiful wife Amanda and handsome son Harper, in the Logan Square neighborhood of Chicago, Illinois.

I'm talkative, easy going, adventurous, and I thrive in taking the lead. I love old music, spending time with my son, and making people laugh.

My story collided with photography through education in video production in high school and college. After being around photography, I fell in love. The process of creating and learning how to make better images of people's love keeps me in love with what I do.

I love weddings because of you and your story.

I believe that as people, marriage is one of the most beautiul things we can partake in, and I love people. From nervous grooms, to shy flower girls, and choked up fathers, it's the you and those around you that make your wedding day.That's why I thrive in photographing the details and interactions of a wedding, as well as the souls around them, making it all happen.
